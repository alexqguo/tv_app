Notes

- Models
	- user
		- has_many: shows (through user_shows), episode_views
	- session
	- show
		- name, description?, active (bool), release_date
		- has_many: users (through user_shows), seasons
	- user_show
		- user_id, show_id
		- belongs_to: user, show
	- season
		- show_id
		- has_many: episodes
		- belongs_to: show
	- episode
		- name, season_id, release_date
		- has_many: episode_views
		- belongs_to: season
	- episode_view
		- user_id, episode_id, in_progress (bool)
		- belongs_to: user, episode
